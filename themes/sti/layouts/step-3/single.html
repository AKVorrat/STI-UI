While the delivery of specialised services is allowed under the Regulation, they cannot be offered as a replacement for Internet access services. Otherwise, ISPs would be conducting price discrimination practices. In its 2012 guidelines, BEREC stated that price discrimination practices offering access to a limited part of the Internet would have a negative effect on consumers' rights.
